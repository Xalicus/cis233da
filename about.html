About Kevin Ward

Life and family

I've been creating websites for over 19+ years, since I was in high school. I went to a Tech School called East Valley Institute of Technology, or EVIT for short. I would go to my normal classes then for half of the day, I'd take a bus ride to EVIT and learn to code webpages and other cool 3D images and animation type stuff. It was a nice learning experience, and I would recommend it to anyone who can do it.

I met my wife at an anime group we both went to, just because we knew some of the members who always went on Friday nights, and they invited us to go. The Friday before Memorial Day 2004, I found out who she was from the online forum they hosted, that we were talking together on for a few weeks.

My wife gave birth to Artemis last year and she has been a shining light as she brings many smiles and giggly laughter in our lives.
